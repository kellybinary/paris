Binary.com is the leading binary options and FX trading group, founded in 1999 and with offices in Malta, Malaysia, Japan, and now Paris. The company offers a wide range of binary options and other derivatives products to an international client base via its award-winning platform. Binary.com offers derivatives contracts with durations ranging from intraday to 1 year, on a wide range of financial instruments including stock indices, FX rates, commodities, and cryptocurrencies.

What We Do

Development of option pricing methodologies

The derivative contracts offered by Binary.com require complex pricing algorithms to adequately model the financial market skew, kurtosis, and microstructure, which can vary considerably depending on the underlying instrument and time horizon of the derivative. By studying various sources of market data, such as implied volatility surfaces, historical price feeds, sources of market news and economic events, IntegralSigma SARL develops pricing models for various derivatives contracts that are both robust enough to withstand a live trading environment and also fast enough to compute to be able to be deployed to Binary.com’s high-traffic trading infrastructure.

Financial market data feed management systems

Financial market data feeds used in a live trading environment require robust implementation in regards to latency, error-detection, failover, and audit trails. Tasks with the development of such systems, IntegralSigma SARL works with high-frequency data feeds, low-latency databases, and implements complex data feed analysis algorithms designed to improve the quality and robustness of the feeds used by the Binary.com trading platforms.

News and economic events modelling

In an increasingly fast-paced world, financial markets are roiled by news events coming in 24/7 from all four corners of the world. To ensure that the Binary.com options prices are properly calibrated to the news events affecting the markets, IntegralSigma SARL models news and economic events and develops artificial-intelligence systems to automatically adjust option prices to best reflect market conditions.

Risk management systems development

With over 500,000 transactions per day, the Binary.com platform is one of the world’s largest and most complex derivatives trading operations. With thousands of derivatives in its book at any given point of time, risk analysis of the book requires the development of complex realtime systems able to monitor visualize the book’s exposure across various dimensions.

Competitive analysis systems

Binary.com strives to offer derivatives contracts to the retail investing public at prices that rival those available to professional investors in the interbank market. IntegralSigma SARL benchmarks the pricing algorithms it develops against prices available in the interbank OTC market for comparable derivatives.

Our team

Our team is comprised of quantitative analysts and financial markets data scientists sourced from France’s top engineering schools. The team works closely with Binary.com’s IT and financial markets professionals based in its Malta, Malaysia and Japan offices.
